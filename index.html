  • Developer Certification in Responsive Web Design (300 Hour Certificate)
  • Featured in Boston Voyager Magazine for my Marketing for Restore Body Balance

  • Recipient of Capstone Award 2017, John Flynn Award 2015 (Community Service), Winner of Global Hackathon 2017 (Best App and Overall Design)

These are some of the accomplishments and awards that I've earned throughout the years. I recently received my developer certification in Responsive Web Design. It took me around six months but it was definitely worth it.
